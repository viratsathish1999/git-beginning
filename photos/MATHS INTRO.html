TRIGNOMETRY




TRIGNOMETRY: the branch of mathematics concerned with specific functions of angles and their application to calculations.
There are six functions of an angle commonly used in trigonometry.Their names and abbreviations are sine (sin), cosine (cos),
tangent (tan), cotangent (cot), secant (sec), and cosecant (csc).

ALGEBRA




Algebra: is a branch of mathematics dealing with symbols and the rules for manipulating those symbols. In elementary algebra,
those symbols (today written as Latin and Greek letters) represent quantities without fixed values, known as variables.
